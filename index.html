SYSTEM UNIT CASES & ATX'S SIZE

Cases can easily be distinguished by physical dimensions.





Mini-tower case

Sometimes called the shuttle form factor and often used with portable computers.




Mid-tower case  

     Most of the cases used the midsize tower category.




Full-tower case

 Used with servers and multiple bay systems.




Desktop

      The least popular case because the system takes up more space and they are       difficult to maintain.




Slimline desktop

Fixed system not upgradable.
